Lasagna

Lasagna with a fork

Ingredients

  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Sheet Noodles
  • Onion
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Put pasta sauce in baking dish
  2. Place noodles on top
  3. Sprinkle with onion
  4. Add more pasta sauce on top
  5. Smooth ricotta cheese on top
  6. Bake at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
  7. Sprinkle with parsley and serve
